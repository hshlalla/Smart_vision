Project report and video: length limits
The length of your written report is constrained in terms of word count.  The word count refers to the main content of the work.  You may omit sections like the Abstract (if you are using one) and any Appendices, etc., from the word count.  You also do not need to include figures, tables, headings, section headings, figure or table legends, etc., in the word count.

Your video should be within the length minimum and maximum.  It is very important not to achieve that maximum by using any tools that speed up your video, or speaking extremely fast.  If the examiners can’t understand what you are saying, you will lose marks.  Instead, make appropriate choices about which aspects of your work to include in the video, to address the requirements.

You may not use AI or AI-generated material to construct your video (unless of course this was the topic of your project).
